DJ Companion — Mobile Site Tutorial
=====================================

The companion site lets you log transitions from your phone during a set,
without touching the laptop. It runs as a local web page served by the
desktop app over your WiFi network.


REQUIREMENTS
------------
- Desktop app must be running on your laptop.
- Phone and laptop must be on the same WiFi network.


STEP 1 — Find your laptop's IP address
---------------------------------------
Open a terminal on the laptop and run:

    hostname -I

Use the first address listed (e.g. 192.168.0.39). This is your local IP.


STEP 2 — Open the companion on your phone
------------------------------------------
In Chrome (or any mobile browser), go to:

    http://<your-laptop-ip>:5000

Example: http://192.168.0.39:5000

You should see a searchable list of all your tracks.


STEP 3 (optional) — Add to Home Screen
----------------------------------------
In Chrome on Android:
  1. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen".
  3. Confirm — it will appear as an app icon on your home screen.

This hides the browser chrome so it feels like a native app.


USING THE COMPANION
--------------------

1. SEARCH FOR A TRACK
   Type in the search box to filter your library. All tracks are shown
   on load. Tap a track to select it as the "From" track.

2. ADD A TRANSITION
   After selecting a track, the "Add" tab opens automatically.

   - To: Search and tap the track you're transitioning to.
   - Rating: Tap ★, ★★, or ★★★ (default is ★★).
   - Notes: Optional free-text notes about the transition.
   - Add in both ways: Check this to also save the reverse transition
     (To → From) at the same rating and notes.
   - Tap "Save Transition".

   After saving, the "Transitions" tab opens showing the updated list.

3. VIEW TRANSITIONS FOR A TRACK
   Tap the "Transitions" tab at any time to see all transitions for the
   selected track, split into Outgoing (→) and Incoming (←) sections.

4. GO BACK
   Tap "← Back" to return to the track search.


DESKTOP SYNC
------------
Any transition saved from the phone appears in the desktop app's
List tab automatically within 5 seconds — no manual refresh needed.
